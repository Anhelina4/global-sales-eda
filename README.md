# Global Sales Exploratory Data Analysis

This notebook performs an end-to-end exploratory data analysis on a global sales dataset.

The analysis answers five business questions:

- Company performance - What are the key revenue, profit, and sales volume metrics?
- Revenue & profitability — Which categories, countries, and channels drive the most profit?
- Product popularity — Which products sell best, and does this vary by geography or channel?
- Operational efficiency — How long does order-to-ship take, and where are the bottlenecks?
- Sales dynamics & seasonality — How has revenue trended, and are there seasonal patterns?

Dataset: Three relational tables — orders, products, countries
Stack: Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
Period: 2010–2017
